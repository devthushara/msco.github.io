The Legacy

From the Bank Vault to the Cinnamon Garden

The story of Matara Spice Co. begins with R.A. Lionel Ranawaka. After decades as a Deputy Chief Financial Officer at a major Sri Lankan state bank, Lionel returned to his roots in Malimbada, Matara—the ancestral home of true cinnamon.

What started as a retirement passion on a 4-acre estate soon revealed exceptional quality. Lionel applied the same meticulous attention to detail required in high-level finance to the art of cultivation. His sons, Thushara (Asia) and Ishara (Australia), joined forces to turn this passion into a global venture.

The 50% Profit Pledge

We believe in ethical partnership, not just employment. We proudly share 50% of peeling profits directly with the skilled local artisans who craft our quills. This ensures sustainability for the community in Malimbada.

Our Future Vision: We are expanding our cultivation to include a boutique cinnamon experience villa, where visitors can eventually join us to harvest, peel, and process oil firsthand.

Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our spice origin, shipping, and usage.

What is the difference between your Ceylon Cinnamon and regular Cassia?

The difference is critical for health. Cassia (common supermarket cinnamon) contains high levels of Coumarin (~1%), a compound linked to liver damage if consumed daily.

Our Ceylon Cinnamon (Cinnamomum verum) contains ultra-low Coumarin traces (~0.004%), making it safe for daily wellness routines. It also has a sweeter, more delicate flavor profile compared to the harsh heat of Cassia.

Do you ship internationally? How long does it take?

Yes, we ship worldwide from Colombo, Sri Lanka.

  • Express (DHL/FedEx): 3-7 business days. Ideal for samples and retail orders.
  • Air Freight: 7-10 days. Best for medium wholesale orders (50kg+).
  • Sea Freight: 25-40 days depending on the destination port. Most cost-effective for bulk pallet orders.
We handle all export documentation, including Phytosanitary Certificates and Certificates of Origin.

Is your cinnamon Organic certified?

Yes. We use strictly traditional farming methods on our Malimbada estate. We do not use synthetic pesticides or chemical fertilizers. For bulk B2B orders, we can provide third-party lab analysis reports confirming the purity and organic status of the batch.

What is the shelf life of your products?

Because we mill and pack fresh at the source, our shelf life is superior to retail brands that sit in warehouses for months.

  • Whole Quills: 3-4 years if stored in a cool, dry place.
  • Powder: 1-2 years. We recommend buying smaller batches of powder or grinding quills yourself for maximum potency.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk purchasing?

Absolutely. We supply cafes, bakeries, and wellness brands directly. We offer tiered pricing for orders starting at 5kg up to container loads. Since you are buying directly from the grower, you avoid distributor markups. Please use the Contact Form for a trade quote.

Can I get a sample before placing a large order?

Yes. We understand the need to verify quality. We offer a Sample Pack containing Quills, Powder, and Oil. Contact us via WhatsApp or Email to arrange shipping to your region.

How should I store cinnamon to keep it fresh?

Moisture and light are the enemies of spice. Store your cinnamon in an airtight glass or tin container in a cool, dark cupboard. Do not store it directly over the stove, as heat and humidity will degrade the volatile oils that give cinnamon its flavor.

Do you do Private Label (OEM) for my brand?

Yes. We can pack our premium cinnamon into your branded packaging (jars, pouches, or boxes). We currently support private label clients in the UK and Australia. Minimum Order Quantities (MOQ) apply for custom packaging.

Are your spices tested for quality and purity?

Yes. All our spices undergo rigorous quality control:

  • Source Verification - We only work with trusted estate partners
  • Processing Standards - Clean, modern facilities meeting international food safety standards
  • Laboratory Testing - Third-party testing for purity, moisture content, and contaminants
  • Certifications - Phytosanitary certificates and Certificates of Origin provided for all exports
  • Traceability - Full batch tracking from source to shipment

For wholesale orders, we can provide detailed lab analysis reports and certifications as required by your market.

What's the difference between fresh and dried turmeric/ginger?

Fresh (Rhizome): Ideal for juices, smoothies, and fresh applications. Higher moisture content, vibrant flavor, shorter shelf life. Must be refrigerated or frozen. Perfect for wellness shots and fresh cooking.

Dried (Powder): Long shelf life, concentrated flavor, easier to store and ship. Perfect for curries, golden milk, and baking. Our dried powders are made from premium rhizomes, sun-dried and finely ground.

We can supply both forms based on your needs and shipping requirements.

🌿 Private Cinnamon Garden Experience – What is it?

Our Private Cinnamon Garden Experience is a 3-hour authentic, guided walking tour through our family cinnamon estate in Malimbada, Matara. You'll meet our founder and master cinnamon peelers, witness live demonstrations of traditional Ceylon cinnamon peeling, and explore the working garden where it all happens. This is a real family operation—not a commercialized tourist attraction.

Is there a minimum group size? Can I join solo?

No minimum group size! Solo travelers, couples, families, and groups are all welcome. The experience is personalized based on your group size, and the tour guide will tailor the pace and content to your interests.

When can I book a tour? How much notice do you need?

We offer tours any day, any season—no closed days! We can accommodate tours year-round, whether it's dry season or monsoon.

Booking Notice: Ideally, provide 1+ days notice so we can prepare and coordinate logistics. The more notice you give, the better we can arrange everything for you.

To book, contact us via WhatsApp with your preferred date, group size, and any special requirements.

Is there an age limit or fitness requirement?

No age restrictions! Anyone can join, from children to seniors. The tour is a light jungle walk at a comfortable pace, adaptable to your fitness level. We encourage families, groups of all ages, and multi-generational travelers.

What to Bring: Comfortable hiking shoes are recommended for uneven terrain. The weather is warm and humid—dress accordingly and bring sunscreen, a hat, and insect repellent.

How do I get to the garden? Is hotel pickup available?

Transportation Options:

  • Hotel Pickup: Available on a case-by-case basis. Additional charges may apply depending on distance. We'll coordinate your hotel and ETA before arrival.
  • Your Own Transport: You can arrange your own transportation. We'll provide directions and contact details to coordinate your arrival time.

We exchange contact information before your arrival to ensure smooth coordination and confirm your ETA.

Are there any health or safety considerations?

Important: Please inform us of any allergies, medical conditions, or dietary restrictions ahead of time so our guide can prepare appropriately.

The tour involves light jungle walking on natural terrain. While wildlife (peacocks, deer, wild boars) may be spotted, they are typically non-aggressive. Bring insect repellent and protective clothing. The walk is gentle and not strenuous.

Can I take photos and videos during the tour?

Photography & Recording: Yes! Feel free to take photos and videos for personal memories.

Important Note: This is a private family property. While recording is permitted, please seek permission before photographing individuals or sensitive areas. The landowners retain full ownership of any professional or commercial content shot on the property and have the right to request you stop recording if needed. Always be respectful and ask first—our team will be happy to help!

What if the weather is bad? Can I reschedule?

We operate tours in all seasons and weather conditions. Rain is part of the authentic experience! The garden remains accessible during light rain, and the jungle is even more beautiful.

For extreme weather conditions, we can discuss rescheduling. Flexibility is key, and we'll work with you to find an alternative date that works for your travel plans.

Can I purchase cinnamon and spices during the tour?

Absolutely! You'll have the opportunity to purchase our premium cinnamon quills, powder, leaves, oil, and other Sri Lankan spices directly from the source.

Note: Availability depends on the season and production schedule. If you're interested in specific products or larger quantities, please mention this when booking so we can ensure stock is available or prepare in advance.

Where exactly is Malimbada? How far from main tourist areas?

Malimbada is centrally located in the Matara District, making it easily accessible from all major tourist hubs in the South. Here are the typical travel times:

  • Madiha: 10 minutes by scooter/car
  • Matara Town: 10 minutes by scooter/car
  • SK Town: 15 minutes by scooter/car
  • Mirissa: 20 minutes by scooter/car
  • Weligama: 30 minutes by scooter/car

Good News: We offer free pick-up and drop-off for most tour bookings! This means you don't need to arrange your own transportation. Just let us know your hotel location, and we'll handle the logistics.

Bottom Line: Whether you're staying in Mirissa, Weligama, or anywhere in the South, you can reach Matara Spice Co.'s Cinnamon Garden within 20 minutes or less.

Can I do a walk-in visit? Do I need to book in advance?

Absolutely! Walk-in visits are welcome. However, we recommend giving us at least 2 hours notice so we can prepare the garden, arrange a guide, and ensure a smooth experience for you.

How to arrange a walk-in: Simply message us on WhatsApp 2+ hours before you'd like to visit. We'll confirm availability and coordinate your arrival time. If we have a guide available on short notice, we'll be ready to welcome you!

Best Practice: A pre-booked tour guarantees your spot and allows us to tailor the experience to your interests. But if you're traveling spontaneously and decide on the spot to visit, don't hesitate to reach out via WhatsApp—we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Can I bring friends and make it a public tour? Or do I want a private experience?

Great question! We offer flexible tour options:

  • Private Tour (Just You): Book exclusively for your group—no one else joins. Perfect for families, couples, or groups seeking a personalized experience.
  • Public Tour: If your group is smaller or you're open to meeting other guests, we often run public tours where multiple small groups meet at the garden. This is a wonderful opportunity to connect with other travelers who share your interest in authentic experiences!

Bringing Friends: If you have friends you'd like to bring, that's fantastic! You can book together as one group. If you mention that you're open to a public tour format (meeting other guests), we may even adjust pricing accordingly or match you with other travelers.

When Booking: Simply let us know your preference—whether it's private, public/shared, or flexible—and we'll work with you to arrange the best option. There's no pressure either way!
